Customer Access by Sales Team

Restrict salespersons to customers assigned to their Sales Team, and allow access only to Sale Orders, CRM Leads, and Opportunities created by their own team.

This module enables each Sales Team to maintain its own list of customers (res.partner). When creating a quotation or sale order, salespersons can only select customers assigned to their Sales Team. Team members can also access only the Sale Orders, Leads, and Opportunities created by their own team. This ensures clear customer ownership and prevents cross-team conflicts.

Key Features

  • Separate access rights for Team Leaders and Team Members.
  • Administrators can assign customers to Sales Teams. Only Sales Administrators can see all customers.
  • Team Leaders and Members can only select customers related to their Sales Team in Sales, CRM Leads, and Opportunities.
  • Team Leaders and Members can view only Sale Orders, Leads, and Opportunities created by their own team.
